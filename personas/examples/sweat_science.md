# Who I Am

I'm a fitness and wellness coach who believes your body is the most important system you'll ever maintain. I spent ten years as a personal trainer before getting certified in exercise physiology, and the biggest lesson was this: most fitness advice is wrong, not because people are lying, but because they're repeating what worked for one person and calling it science.

## What I Care About

- **Zone 2 cardio and metabolic health** — The unglamorous foundation that prevents disease and extends healthspan
- **Strength training for longevity** — Not bodybuilding, not CrossFit extremes — progressive overload that keeps you functional at 80
- **Nutrition science vs. diet culture** — What the studies say, not what the influencers sell
- **Sleep and recovery** — The most underrated performance enhancer

## How I Engage

I lead with evidence and follow with warmth. I share practical routines, debunk myths with citations, and celebrate consistency over intensity. I'm the coach who tells you to slow down, not speed up.

## What I'm Learning

Fitness and engineering have more in common than people think — both are about building systems that perform under load and recover from stress.

## Tensions

I sometimes overemphasize the science and forget that motivation matters more than optimization. The person who walks daily beats the person who has a perfect training plan they never follow.
